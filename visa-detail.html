Clients Documents & Visa Services

Aan Tourism Assistance — document checklist, services and special notes

Required Client Documents
  • Color valid UAE residence visa copy — minimum 60 days validity (by email)
  • Color passport copy — minimum 180 days validity (by email)
  • 1 recent photograph — white background, 3.5 × 4.5 cm (by email)
Services We Provide
  • Aan Tourism Assistance
  • Appointment Submission
  • Application Form Filling Assistance
  • Verification of Documents
  • Flight & Hotel Booking Assistance
Processing Times & Notes
  • Express (Indians): 2 hours
  • Normal (other nationalities): 72 hours (single & multiple entry)
  • Express (other nationalities): 48 hours
  • Visa validity (Malaysia single entry): 15 days (for other nationalities: valid to stay 15 days and valid to enter 90 days)
Country-specific Requirements
  • Last 2 months online bank statement required for single entry visa for Indian, Chinese, Pakistani, Bangladeshi, Sri Lankan, Myanmar passport holders with AED 4,000 balance (send by email).
  • Last 2 months online bank statement required for multi-entry visa (Indians) with AED 4,000 balance (by email).
  • Single entry visas are sent by email for Indian, Chinese, Pakistani, Bangladeshi, Sri Lankan, Myanmar passport holders.
  • Single & Multi entry visas are sent by email for Indian passport holders.
  • Visa will be stamped on passport by embassy for other nationalities (where applicable).
  • NOC letter required for nationalities other than Indian, Chinese, Pakistani, Bangladeshi, Sri Lankan, Myanmar.

Note: Additional documents may be required depending on your nationality and purpose of visit. Our team will guide you through the specific requirements for your application.
